Process of Creation

I created this product to resemble that of a website for a charity organization against animal cruelty. I used various other charity websites to model my own after and used the skills in HTML and CSS I gained from the course at the school to create the structure and style of the website. I modeled my style after other charity organizations and modern websites that I believed looked good. I used websites such as geeksforgeeks.org when I was stuck or forgot how to do something such as make a border for a div. This will not be included in my Works Cited as, along with the other websites I used, I did not cite it or any of it's information anywhere on my site and googling is a very common thing for programmers. If googling help during programming was something that had to be cited, everything ever programmed would have a very long list of geeksforgeeks, stackoverflow, and w3schools links.
